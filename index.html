The James Webb Telescope's Latest Discoveries

The James Webb Space Telescope has captured unprecedented images of deep space, revealing galaxies that formed just millions of years after the Big Bang. This changes our understanding of the early universe.

By observing in the infrared spectrum, Webb can pierce through thick dust clouds that previously obscured our view of stellar nurseries.
